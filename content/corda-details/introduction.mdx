In the previous module, we learned how to use existing libraries. In this module we are going to learn about some details of the core parts of the Corda system. In particular:

* What are attachments in Corda.
* What are reference states.
* What are time windows.
* What are services.
* What is the structure of transactions.
* What are oracles, and how to implement them.
